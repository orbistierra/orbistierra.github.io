OUR STORY

Being your ideal choice of lodging,The Orbis Tierra Hotel is a four star hotel that offers one of a kind luxurious lodging services and a unique ambiance, letting the guest to enjoy a day having a feeling of being at home.

The Hotel will offer facilities for guest such as: Business centers, Function rooms, Grand Ballroom, swimming pools, sports court, boutique shops, gym, and spas. The establishment will not only give services to the hotel guest but will open the restaurant doors to the customers who want to experience a fine dining and delicious meals that the restaurant will offer, the owners wanted to encourage the guest to try different Filipino cuisines.

Devoted to give a Filipino hospitable way of accommodating, Orbis Tierra Hotel provides you a highly standard of delivering great services and a wide range of facilities to ensure that your stay is as comfortable as possible.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Mission

Orbis Tierra aims to provide an unforgettable unique experience through having a comfortable and a good quality of accommodation that will have the passion for exceeding the expectations of our guest through a whole hearted service.

Vision

To fill the country with light and warmth of hospitality service and will be the most distinguished independent luxurious Filipino hotel that will showcase the four natural elements which can fulfill the unexpressed wishes and needs of our guests.
